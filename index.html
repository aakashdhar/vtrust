What We Do

An open-box item looks and feels brand new, although its packaging can sometimes show some wear and tear. Demo models from retail locations that just can’t be sold as “brand new” anymore. Some of the products may have never even been powered on, but have merely been removed from their packaging at some point. As a result, these products are heavily discounted even though you can expect them to function as if they were new.

ValueTrust.com professionally tests all open-box products to ensure they are fully functional, and that they look and feel brand new. At ValueTrust.com we only sell used items that pass our rigorous testing process, and we stand by our promise to give our customers the best price on every one of these items.
